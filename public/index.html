About Me

I'm Mohammed Aflah UP, a passionate and dedicated full-stack developer currently learning and growing in the field of web development. Along with my studies, I've also completed an internship where I gained hands-on experience in real-world projects. I specialize in the MERN Stack — MongoDB, Express.js, React, and Node.js. After completing my higher secondary education, I dedicated a focused gap year (CAP) to sharpening my development skills. I now work at UpCode Software Lab as a full-time developer, where I actively contribute to building dynamic and efficient web applications.

I build responsive websites that look great on all devices, and I always follow best coding practices to ensure clean, scalable, and maintainable code. I have solid experience in HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and I work confidently with modern tools and frameworks like Tailwind CSS, React, and Node.js.

My key strengths include problem-solving, curiosity-driven learning, and adaptability. I'm always looking for opportunities to work on exciting projects, collaborate with amazing people, and grow as a developer.
